Julia Advanced (Fracktal work)

The Julia Advanced 3D Printer, developed by Fracktal Works, is a professional-grade 3D printer designed for versatility, reliability, and ease of use. The printer offers automatic calibration to ensure optimal results and quick setup. It features a 3.5-inch touchscreen for easy operation and allows quick-swap nozzles, reducing downtime. The sturdy metallic chassis ensures durability, suitable for industrial applications like jigs, fixtures, and low-volume production.

Original Prusa XL

The Original Prusa XL is a large-format FFF 3D printer with a 360 x 360 x 360 mm build area. Available with one or five heads, it supports efficient dual-material and multicolor printing using two independent heads. This advanced design enables faster production of intricate 3D objects, making it ideal for both single-material and complex, multi-material projects.

FormBox Vacuum Forming mc

The FormBox uses a high-airflow steel mesh and any home vacuum cleaner to generate industrial-grade suction. This powerful system molds sheets with precision, capturing intricate crevices and fine details effortlessly. Perfect for rapid prototyping and creative projects, it delivers professional-grade results in a compact, easy-to-use design.

PROXXON FF 500/BL CNC MILLING MACHINE

The Proxxon FF 500 CNC is a high-precision milling machine ideal for milling, drilling, and countersinking operations. Designed for quiet, vibration-free performance, it offers a wide speed range to accommodate various materials and tasks. Its CNC capabilities ensure accurate, repeatable results, making it perfect for precision machining projects requiring reliability and versatility.

PROXXON PD 400 CNC LATHE MACHINE

The Proxxon PD 400 CNC is a precision lathe that automates machining via software, enabling repeatable operations. It handles tasks like turning, facing, drilling, taper-turning, knurling, threading, and parting off with high accuracy. Ideal for intricate projects, it ensures consistent results and versatility across multiple machining processes, making it a reliable choice for precision engineering needs.
